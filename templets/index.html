Our Journey

From a small idea to a platform that impacts thousands of women, here's how SakhiRise evolved over time.

2018

The Beginning

SakhiRise was founded with a vision to create a comprehensive platform that addresses the unique challenges faced by women in India.

2019

First 1,000 Users

We reached our first milestone of 1,000 active users and launched our period tracking and health monitoring features.

2020

Safety Features Added

In response to the pandemic, we expanded our platform to include safety alerts, emergency contacts, and location sharing.

2021

Financial Tools Launch

We introduced financial planning tools, resources for women entrepreneurs, and partnered with financial institutions.

2022

Educational Expansion

Expanded our educational resources, added career guidance, and launched mentorship programs connecting women with industry leaders.

2023

10,000+ Users

Reached 10,000+ active users, launched our mobile app, and expanded our services to rural areas through partnerships.

Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my personal health data?

Your privacy is our top priority. All health data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent. You have complete control over what information you choose to store and share.

Is SakhiRise available on mobile devices?

Yes! SakhiRise is fully responsive and works on all devices. We also offer native mobile apps for iOS and Android, giving you access to all features even when you're offline, with automatic syncing when you reconnect.

How can I connect with other women on the platform?

Our Community section allows you to join interest-based groups, participate in discussions, and connect with mentors. All community interactions are moderated to ensure a safe, supportive environment for all members.

Are the educational resources free to access?

Most of our educational content is completely free. We offer additional premium courses and workshops at affordable prices, with scholarships available for those who need financial assistance.
